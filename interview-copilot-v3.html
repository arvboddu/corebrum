Interview Copilot
v2 · MEETING MODE

Captures audio from Teams, Zoom, Google Meet — or your microphone — and generates real-time AI answers.

01
Tab Audio Capture (Recommended for Meetings)
Use Share Tab mode — open your meeting in a separate tab, then share that tab here. Captures all meeting audio (interviewer + you) without extra software.
02
Microphone Mode (Fallback)
Captures only your microphone. Use this if tab capture isn't available in your browser, or for in-person interviews.
03
Configure LLM
Runs on Ollama (free, local). Start Ollama with: OLLAMA_ORIGINS=* ollama serve then pull a model: ollama pull llama3.2
💡 Best workflow for Teams / Zoom / Meet: Open the meeting in a separate Chrome/Edge tab. Then in this tab click Start Capture → choose Share a Tab → select the meeting tab and tick Share tab audio. This captures all speech with no plugins required.
